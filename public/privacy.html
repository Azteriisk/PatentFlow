PatentFlow

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 7, 2026

PatentFlow is built with a commitment to privacy, data minimization, and local-first architecture. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in the PatentFlow application.

1. Information Collection and Transmission

We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information.

  • No Accounts: PatentFlow does not require registration, accounts, or profile creation.
  • No Analytics or Telemetry: The application does not contain tracking scripts, third-party analytics (e.g., cookies, telemetry), or crash-reporting services that collect device profiles.
  • Local Storage: All user preferences, bookmarks, and USPTO/TSDR API configurations are saved locally on your device within the sandbox container (localStorage) and never uploaded to our servers.

2. Third-Party Services (USPTO & TSDR APIs)

PatentFlow acts as a client utility to fetch patent and trademark information directly from the United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO). When you perform searches:

  • Your search terms are transmitted directly to the official USPTO endpoints (api.uspto.gov and tsdrapi.uspto.gov) using your locally configured API keys.
  • These requests are subject to the USPTO's privacy policy and standard web connection logs. No intermediary servers intercept or monitor your queries.

3. Security

Because all data remains strictly local to your device, the security of your stored configuration relies entirely on the security of your local operating system container and device. We recommend securing your devices with standard passwords/biometrics.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted by updating the static policy page within the application package and web deployment.

5. Contact

If you have any questions or feedback about this privacy policy, please contact the developer via the official repository.
